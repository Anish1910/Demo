Our Story

Building a sustainable future, one thrifted piece at a time.

How It Started

Thrift Better was born from frustration. In 2022, our founder realized that fast fashion was everywhere‚Äîcheap, disposable, and destroying the planet. Simultaneously, incredible vintage and secondhand pieces were being overlooked, undervalued, and hard to find.

We started as a simple Instagram account sharing curated thrifted pieces. Within months, we realized there was massive demand from Gen-Z and young adults who wanted to thrift smarter‚Äîwithout the haul culture, without the waste, just genuine, intentional fashion.

Today, Thrift Better is a community platform dedicated to making secondhand fashion accessible, stylish, and socially conscious. We curate incredible pieces, educate about thrifting, and make it easier for people to buy and consign pre-loved clothes.

Our mission: Prove that the best fashion is already out there. You just need to know where to look.
