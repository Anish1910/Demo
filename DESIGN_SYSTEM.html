Thrifting is shopping for secondhand clothes and goods at thrift stores, vintage shops, or online platforms. It's about finding unique pieces, saving money, and reducing fashion waste.

Thrifting is sustainable, affordable, and gives you access to unique, one-of-a-kind pieces. You're reducing fashion waste while building an original wardrobe.

Animations & Transitions

Smooth, subtle animations that enhance user experience.

Transition Speeds

  • Fast: 0.2s (hover effects)
  • Normal: 0.3s (default transitions)
  • Smooth: 0.5s (major layout changes)

Animation Examples

Fade In: Elements fade in smoothly on page load

Slide Up: Cards slide up slightly on hover with shadow

Height Expand: Accordion smoothly expands/collapses
